Who We Are

Your recruitment partner — built for Nigerian businesses

MyTalentConnect Ltd is a Lagos-based HR solutions firm on a mission to take the stress out of hiring. We handle sourcing, screening and shortlisting end-to-end, so you only ever meet candidates who are truly ready to deliver.

  • Pre-vetted candidates, no noise, no wasted interviews
  • End-to-end service from job ad to onboarding
  • Deep understanding of the Nigerian talent market
  • Personalised support, not a one-size-fits-all agency
  • Serving companies of all sizes across Nigeria
